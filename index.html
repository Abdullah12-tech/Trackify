Frequently asked questions.

What is this expense tracker ?

This expense tracker is a simple and efficient tool designed to help you manage your income and expenses, track spending habits, and stay on top of your financial goals.

How do I add an expense?

To add an expense, simply log in to your account, navigate to the "Add Expense" section, enter the amount, category, and description, and click "Save."

Can I track my income as well?

Yes! Our tracker allows you to log both income and expenses, helping you maintain a clear overview of your financial status.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use encryption and security measures to ensure your financial data remains private and protected.

Can I edit or delete an expense after adding it?

Yes, you can edit or delete any expense from your records at any time through the dashboard.
